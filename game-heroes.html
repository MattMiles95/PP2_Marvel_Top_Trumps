To experience epic battles between your favourite heroes and villains, please turn your mobile device side on - or even better, load this game on your tablet or PC!

How to Play?

  • Click 'FIGHT' to deal the playing cards
  • Each card has 5 stats with a rating from 1 - 10:
    1. Power
    2. Agility
    3. Intelligence
    4. Fighting Skills
    5. Battle IQ
  • The goal is to pick a stat with a higher score than your opponent's
  • Your opponent's stats are hidden, so you'll have to make an educated guess as to which stat your character can win on
  • To play a stat, simply click on the corresponding button to the right of your card
  • The winner is the first player to reach a score of 7 - so pick wisely!
  • Your deck only holds 14 cards - careful you don't run out...
  • Good luck, True Believers!
